Events at Biswa-Tex




Biswa-Tex's Foundation Day

Since the very first year of our establishment we have been celebrating this once day, the foundation day of this firm(1st of April), it always is entertaining to co-memorate the past with their collegues, Biswa-Tex provides them the chance. Starts off as a domestic cultural festival. Making a stage for talents exhibited by the employees and their families, each participant is awarded for their effective participation in the concert. The lunch on that day is complimentary and every employee and their families are invited. After a sumptuous meal there awaits a concert by called upon stars. Finally the day ends with a token of love and gratitude from the Biswa-Tex family to their employees as an answer to their year long hard work. Gifts are graded according to seniority.

Annual Trip

Every year Biswa-Tex arranges a scheduled trip in the winters to any nearby location, the cost of this trip is beared fully by the management, another initiative of the firm's management to express gratitude to its employees for the effort they put in. The trip includes free fooding, lodging and transportation. We will care for you if you ensure to care for us. It is another process by which this firm ensures harmonical co-existance between the employees.

Other Events

Among other events we have celebration of different kind of pujas. Vishwakarma Puja, Saraswati Puja and few others. On all these days the lunch is organised by the firm. On the day of Viswakarma puja it is a real feat of the members. In all these we ensure to invite all the family members of the emplouyees.

AWARDS

Along with all these, different awards are allocated for the employees of Biswa-Tex graded upon their efficiency. There are awards such as :-

  • Employee of the Month;
  • Employee of the Year;
  • Most regular Employee;
  • Most diligent employee;
  • Most productive employee;

Its nice to keep up a competitive spirit among the co-workers, aided by this initiative of ours.
